About Me

Later, I did my business on an old Chromebook, on which I learned Python, began playing the game torn.space, which is how I got my name, and began my presence on the web.

I grew my Arduino collection, aquired a few single board computers, and got better at programming, and eventually, here we are.
In case you couldn't tell, this all took place over about 6 years.

Day/Month names SUCK! 

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. Also, who the hell came up with weekday names?!

Maybe sorta a blog blog (Post 1)

Hello, I am writing this on a very sweaty (is that how you’d describe it?) Monday evening, on the last day of February. (No, it’s not a leap year.)

Now, with that out of the way, I have something to say.

Day/Month names suck.

I hate how they’re always slipping in extra letters that nobody even says. Take a look at some examples below, to prove how much spell check pisses me off about this.

  • Wednesday. (pronounced “wensday”)
  • February. (pronounced “febuary”)
  • June/August (I take no issue with the actual spelling, but why did those Roman rulers bother naming months after themselves, why not pick cooler things, like himself! Keep reading to find out some crap on why naming yourself is the best.)
  • Any other ones with more than 3 syllables.

Wednesday

I hate this one because Wednesday has so many unnecessary letters! Think about it, do you really hear most people going “Oh, today’s Wed-ne-sday!”? No! If me and most people I’m around pronounce it one way, keep the spell check and dictionary out of it, google/grammarly/

spell checker of choice/weird person who manually looks at a dictionary for spelling. (Don’t @ me on this one, I pronounce this one way, but I actually spell it the dictionary-approved way, because, y’know, I have to keep my grades up in English, as I am your typical Joe Honor Roll with a GPA hovering slightly under a 4. (Think 3.8, maybe.) We’re getting off topic, so on to the next one!

February

Now this one, this one isn’t too bad, It only has one letter I deem unfit, which is the ‘r’ after the “feb” part. Fun fact, “feb” is kinda like “fed.” which is kinda like the “feds,” who are kinda after me for being $49 million deep in crippling debt! (yay) Anyways, next!

August/June

Like I said in the list above, I take no issue with the actual spelling, but why did those Roman rulers bother naming months after themselves, why not pick cooler things, like himself! Here’s why naming yourself is better than naming anything else:

You have no limits as to what you can call yourself!

You can change it back so you don’t sound like an idiot!

You don’t have to make permanent changes to a widely-used calendar system!

Conclusion

I hope that you are now enlightened in how names for days and months suck. Keep in mind this is only mostly a joke, and that the rest of the days and months are pretty okay.

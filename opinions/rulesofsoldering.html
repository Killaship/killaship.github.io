RULES OF SOLDERING*



  • A fume hood is not mandatory, this ain't a science lab, just don't be stupid, don't inhale the fumes. (desk fans!)

  • Wear glasses of some kind to prevent the eyes getting crispy. If you can't afford a pair of sunglasses, but you can afford a soldering station, then you might wanna adjust your priorites. Anyways, if you don't have safety glasses, some alternatives are the Safety Squints, or the Safety Dance.

  • Try and keep things from setting on fire, and solder away from the smoke alarm, if you don't have a fan.

  • Wash your hands, dumbass. Lead poisoning isn't good.

  • Spongey sponges are your friend!
